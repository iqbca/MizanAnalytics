\# Mizan Analytics



\*\*Institutional Investment Reporting Platform\*\*



Mizan Analytics is a modular investment reporting platform built to automate portfolio analytics, benchmark comparison, risk measurement and executive reporting for institutional investment portfolios.



The project simulates a real-world reporting workflow used by pension funds, asset managers and investment consultants.
